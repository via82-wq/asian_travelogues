Persian Travelogues: Movement of Ideas

Texts

About This Project

This digital humanities project examines Persian and Urdu travelogues to understand the movement of ideas related to nationalism, politics, and culture.

Corpus

Primary text: Naser Khosrow's Safarnama

Research Questions

  • How do travelogues reflect cultural exchange?
  • What political ideas emerge in travel narratives?

Methodology

- The source text was digitized via OCR from archive.org. I have retained these mistakes and transcription artifacts from the original scan simply because there were far too many corrections to make. - I have not distinguished semantic markup between towns and cities since this difference in markup was not significant to this project. Therefore, "city" attributes have mostly been used elsewhere, but have also been used interchangeably with "town" attributes.
