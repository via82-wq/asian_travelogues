Cultural Exchange in Persian Travelogues

About This Project

This project examines cultural and political exchange between Europe and Asia through the lens of a 19th-century Persian travelogue: The Diary of H.M. the Shah of Persia During His Tour Through Europe in A.D. 1873, translated by J.W. Redhouse.

Research Questions

What ideas — cultural, political, industrial — were exchanged between Europe and Asia during this period? How did a Persian monarch perceive and document European modernity?

Methodology

The text has been encoded in XML to identify and analyze patterns of exchange, including geographic locations, cultural observations, ethnic and national identities, key figures, and diplomatic gatherings.
