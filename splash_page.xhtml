About This Project

This digital humanities project examines Persian and Urdu travelogues to understand the movement of ideas related to nationalism, politics, and culture.

Corpus

Primary text: Naser Khosrow's Safarnama

Research Questions

  • How do travelogues reflect cultural exchange?
  • What political ideas emerge in travel narratives?
